Baptist Youth Summer Camp 2016

Dear Camper

We are so excited that you will be attending this year’s summer camp. We pray that not only will it be camp were you meet new people, make amazing memories but also one where you connect closer with God. Our theme for this year’s camp is No Compromise, taken from Daniel 3:16-18. No Compromise presents an opportunity for Young People to consider Biblical absolutes which we all stand for. In a world latent with relativism and widespread bankruptcy of character, rife with corruption, confusion with identity, sexual immorality, collapsing families, and alternate worship, there is a need to re-establish who we are and the centre that holds us together - Jesus Christ. Evidently, at every turning point of human history God has used young people, Joseph, Joshua, David, Daniel, Jeremiah, Mary and so on, who were willing to stand up and be counted. Its time to stand or else we will fall for anything!
